VaR as a Risk Measure

VaR is one of the most popular tools used in risk management and is incorporated within the Basel Accord for banking supervision. VaR is used by central counterparties (CCPs) for purposes of determining initial margin.

Our core solution is VaR.

Risk is calculated below for long positions in various asset classes and sectors using HVaR (antithetic HVaR applies to short positions). These values are similar to initial margins required by exchanges/brokers/banks and vary according to HVaR parameters and discretionary factors. Parameters used for this model can be viewed by clicking on the graph icons. For example, a 99th percentile indicates that 1% of the observations will be below the HVaR line. Stress VaR, required by banking regulators is also shown.
